Visual Systems & Experiments

Investigating how high-density information (emotion, history, narrative) is compressed into visual formats.

Nüshu: Material Systems

Fieldwork Installation

Experiment: How does the materiality of Nüshu (women's script)—fabric, tears, ribbons—affect the transmission of its message? Created a "boudoir" installation to contextualize the script beyond its text.

Qiaopi: Narrative Pipeline

Stop-Motion Paper Cutout

Experiment: Optimizing a frame-by-frame production pipeline to animate archival history. Focused on paper textures to mimic the fragility of the original letters.

LOFTER: Feedback Loops

106k+ Reach Serial Narrative

Experiment: A 3-year longitudinal study in character design and audience retention. Developed a consistent visual language that sustained long-term community engagement.
